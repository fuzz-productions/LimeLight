LimeLight is a help engine for Android. It allows application developers to easily create interactive, context-driven hints, tooltips, and tutorials for their end users.

Drag around helpful objects

LimeLight lets you drag objects that point out and describe elements of your app. In Edit Mode, the screen elements are highlighted so you can easily distinguish which objects are referring to them.

Customize the look of your tooltips

LimeLight allows you to change the text, text color, text size, background color, icon, and animation of every tooltip. You can also change the font for all tooltips at once.

Record and play all of your tooltips

LimeLight's playback system allows you to have a timeline of helpful objects. The objects can either play one after another or wait until the user performs an action. LimeLight can also perform transitions between each tooltip. For instance, you can set the drawer to open after a particular object finishes playing.

Why limit yourself to just screenshots and vague instructions? LimeLight works on top of your app, above its layout, to display rich, real-time tutorial content.
